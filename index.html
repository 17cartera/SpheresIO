SpheresIO.net


How to Play:

  • Select nodes with left-click, then click on a node to move units to it.
  • Capturing nodes to increases your unit cap and spawn rates.
  • To win, capture three out of the four cores by capturing all of the nodes orbiting them.

Controls:

  • Use the mouse to move your units between nodes.
  • Use WASD or the mouse to move the camera.
  • You can zoom in or out using the scroll wheel.
  • Use E and Q to increase or decrease the amount of units sent per move.
  • Hold spacebar to box select.
  • Press X to deselect the node underneath your mouse.
