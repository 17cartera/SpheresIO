Spheres.io


How to Play:

  • Left-click on a node to select it, drag your mouse across multiple nodes to select multiple.
  • Release or press left-click again on a target node to send units from your selected nodes to the target.
  • Units will fight enemy units and attempt to capture territory, with more units dealing more damage and capturing faster.
  • Capture nodes to spawn more units and increase your unit capacity.
  • Use WASD or mouse dragging to move the camera, zoom in and out using the scroll wheel.
  • Q and E can be used to quickly change the percentage of units sent per move.
  • Hold spacebar to select all nodes in a box.
