Spheres.io


How to Play:

  • Your objective is to capture as many nodes on the map as possible.
  • Move the camera using WASD, or by dragging the screen with the mouse.
  • Left-click on a node to select it, drag your mouse across multiple nodes to select them all.
  • Release your mouse on a target to send half of your units from all selected nodes to the target.
  • Double-click on a target to send all of your units from the selected nodes.
  • Your units will automatically attack enemy units. If they eliminate all enemies on a node, they will begin to capture it.
  • Captured nodes spawn more units and increase your unit capacity.
